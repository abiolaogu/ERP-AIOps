## 9. Key Assumptions and Risks

### 9.1 Revenue Assumptions
- New customer acquisition accelerates with each additional AE (6-month ramp)
- NRR declines gradually from 142% to 130% as customer base matures (conservative)
- Tier mix shifts toward Enterprise as customers adopt AIDD Tier 3
- No revenue from APAC until 2029

### 9.2 Cost Assumptions
- Engineering salaries inflate 5% annually (Austin market)
- Cloud infrastructure costs decrease 15% annually per resource (scale + optimization)
- Sales productivity reaches full ramp at 6 months (industry average for enterprise)
- G&A scales sub-linearly with revenue

### 9.3 Risk Factors
- Slower enterprise adoption: mitigated by POC-led sales (72% win rate post-POC)
- Margin compression from GPU costs: mitigated by model optimization and inference caching
- Competitive response: mitigated by architectural moat (18-month head start on remediation)
- Macro downturn: mitigated by cost-reduction value proposition (reduces headcount needs)
