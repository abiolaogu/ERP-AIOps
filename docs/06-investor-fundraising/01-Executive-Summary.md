## The Opportunity

Enterprise IT operations is broken. The average enterprise runs 1,200+ microservices, generates 15 million events per day, and relies on 6-8 fragmented monitoring tools that produce more noise than signal. Operations teams drown in alerts -- 73% of which are false positives -- while genuine incidents cascade undetected. Mean Time to Resolution (MTTR) averages 4.2 hours, costing Fortune 500 companies $9,000 per minute of downtime.

Sovereign AIOps eliminates this chaos through autonomous IT operations. Our platform ingests telemetry from every infrastructure layer, correlates events in real-time via a shared Redpanda event bus, detects anomalies using proprietary ML models, and executes remediation runbooks without human intervention -- all governed by a three-tier guardrail framework (AIDD) that ensures safety at every autonomy level.

The AIOps market is valued at **$18 billion** (Gartner, 2025) and growing at **25% CAGR**, reaching $55 billion by 2030. Three secular tailwinds drive this expansion:

1. **Cloud-native complexity**: Kubernetes adoption has increased infrastructure entities 40x per application

## Competitive Differentiation

Sovereign AIOps is the **only AIOps platform natively integrated into ERP operations**. While PagerDuty ($390M revenue) alerts humans, Datadog ($2.1B) visualizes metrics, and Dynatrace ($1.3B) traces transactions, none of them close the loop with autonomous remediation governed by enterprise-grade guardrails.

**Five structural advantages:**

1. **Autonomous Remediation**: Not just detection -- automated execution of validated runbooks with rollback safety nets
2. **ERP-Native Integration**: Shared Redpanda event bus enables zero-delay correlation between IT infrastructure events and business process impacts
3. **AIDD Guardrail Framework**: Three-tier autonomy model (Monitor, Suggest, Act) lets enterprises progressively increase automation confidence
4. **Unified Topology**: Real-time dependency mapping across infrastructure, application, and business layers eliminates blind spots
5. **Noise Reduction Engine**: Proprietary event correlation reduces alert volume by 73%, eliminating the top pain point in IT operations
