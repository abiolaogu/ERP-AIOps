# ERP-AIOps Model-Based Business Plan

## Strategic Objective

Establish ERP-AIOps as the category king platform in SRE organizations and platform operations teams in mid-market and enterprise through a disciplined scale model that links product strategy, GTM execution, and financial outcomes.

## Growth Architecture

- Verticalized demand creation and account-based enterprise motion
- Partner-assisted implementation for speed-to-value
- Expansion-led account growth via module and usage attach

## Operating Model

| Function | Operating Priority | KPI |
|---|---|---|
| Product | release value-bearing capabilities each quarter | feature activation and expansion conversion |
| GTM | predictable pipeline and win-rate quality | pipeline coverage and CAC payback |
| Customer success | retention and expansion compounding | NRR and gross retention |
| Finance | capital efficiency and runway control | burn multiple and forecast accuracy |

1. Win repeatability in core ICP segments.
2. NRR expansion through category-specific module adoption.
